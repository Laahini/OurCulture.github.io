Dia De Los Muertos

Dia de los Muertos is also known as the Day of the Dead and is a Mexican holiday that celebrates your ancestors and family members that have passed away. It is the most important festival in Mexico and requires lots of preparations like laying a trail of marigolds and setting up an ofrenda, altar, for the dead. There are lots of food and music played and created for the dead. The dead come back to visit their family members on the Day of the Dead by following the marigold petals.

Juan's Story
