Raksha Bandhan

Raksha Bandhan is a festival celebrated in India to honor the sister-brother relationship. This festival is usually celebrated with sisters tying rakhis, bracelets, to their brothers to protect them from evil spirits and the brothers giving their blessings and support, along with money. Raksha means protection, whilst Bandhan is the verb to tie, henceforth the rakhis of the festival.
