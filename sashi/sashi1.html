About OurCulture

OurCulture was created by Laahini A as a hackathon project and initiative in 2021. Within the past few years, there has been an increase in discrimination and violence against peoples of different religions, cultures, races and peoples. This includes the AAPI movement and BLM movement which had picked up in order to combat racism against Asians and African Anericans. OurCulture supports such movements and spreads it via cultural education.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate others about different cultures across the globe to prevent discrimination. We also aim to create a community of individuals that support each other and are there for each other as support. We hope that through our educational games, people of all ages will learn more about cultures and how to appreciate them. Many who say harmful words about a group of peoples often do not know a lot about their culture/race/etc. That's why we focus on educating people to repress any discrimination.
