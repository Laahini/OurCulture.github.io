Welcome to OurCulture

OurCulture is an organization that focuses on promoting cultural awareness and diversity through education. There are people of all nationalities, religions, cultures, races in our world and it is high time that we stop discrimination because of it. Many wars and disasters have resulted due to discrimination against a certain group of people. It is now the time to stop repeating our past mistakes and work towards a peaceful global atmosphere.
