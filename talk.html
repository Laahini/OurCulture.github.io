Chat OurCommunity

OurCulture wants to create a safe space for you to discuss any discrimination you faced, meet other people of different cultures and create a center of live learning. Meeting new people and learning from them first-hand is another amazing way to educate others about cultures and make them more well known. Our chat system will help OurCulture with our goal of repressing discrimination against certain groups of people as it will provide to be a service where people can discuss their opinions and educate others.

Share Your Work

OurCulture would also love to create a community of creative individuals who can showcase their projects and works on our padlet to spread the word about their culture. You can post drawings, posters, promotions, songs, poems, blog posts, and so much more. We would love to have it as an educational compilation of reosurces for users to interact with. If you would like to display and share your work with the OurCulture community, please make a post on the padlet and feel free to place your personal links and references in the captions.
We can't wait to see what you make!
