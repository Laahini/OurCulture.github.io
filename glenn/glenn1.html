Blackening of the Bride

Blackening of the Bride is not what it seems. It is a Scottish tradition where family members, friends and relatives splash disgusting things like rotten eggs, tomatoes, and mud on a soon-to-be married couple as a blessing. If the couple is able to survive the humiliation, it is seen as they will have a happy marriage. It's a very messy and fun tradition!
